Pricing FAQ

Common questions about cost and what's included.

How much does dog poop pickup cost in St. Louis? ▼
Tidy Tails charges $70/month for weekly service for 1–2 dogs. That's about $2.30 per day. Three to four dogs is $80/month, and five or more dogs is $90/month — that's the ceiling. One-time spring cleanups start at $75. Bi-weekly visits are $45 each. No per-visit fees, no contracts, no surprises.
Is the price the same no matter how big my yard is? ▼
Yes. Tidy Tails does not charge by yard size. A quarter-acre lot in Florissant and a half-acre lot in Chesterfield both pay the same flat rate. The price depends only on how many dogs you have, not on square footage. This is unusual — most pet waste companies do charge for larger yards.
Are there contracts or cancellation fees? ▼
None. Month-to-month, no minimums, no cancellation fees. If your situation changes — you're moving, traveling, need to pause — just text us. We'll sort it out. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you into anything.
What's included in the monthly price? ▼
Everything: weekly visits on the same day, an "On My Way" text 30–60 minutes before arrival, an "All Done" text when finished, full-yard grid sweep (corners and fence lines included), waste bagged and removed from your property, gate access documented and handled. No setup fees, no fuel surcharges, no hidden anything.
How does Tidy Tails compare in price to other St. Louis services? ▼
Most St. Louis pooper scooper services charge $18–$20 per visit. For weekly service that's $936–$1,040/year. Tidy Tails charges a flat $840/year for 1–2 dogs weekly — saving most customers $96–$460 per year. And unlike per-visit services, your monthly cost is predictable, not variable.
Is the first cleanup really free? ▼
Yes. New customers who sign up for weekly or bi-weekly service get their first cleanup completely free. No credit card required to start. Just text (314) 850-7140 with your address and dog count and we'll get you scheduled.
Can I just get a one-time spring cleanup without committing to monthly service? ▼
Absolutely. One-time cleanups are available starting at $75 for 1–2 dogs. If there's significant winter buildup (3–4 months), pricing may be slightly higher — text us your dog count and we'll give you a quick honest quote. No pressure to convert to monthly, though many customers do after seeing the result.
Do you charge more for multiple dogs? ▼
A small amount, yes — but it caps out quickly. 1–2 dogs: $70/mo. 3–4 dogs: $80/mo. 5+ dogs: $90/mo. After 5 dogs, the price stays at $90 no matter how many more you have. Most 3-dog households are still paying less per dog than they would with national franchise pricing for a single-dog account.
