Common Questions

Is the first cleanup really free?
Yes — 100% free, no strings attached. Your first yard cleanup is on us when you sign up for any recurring service (weekly, biweekly, or monthly). If you don't love it, you can cancel before the second visit and owe nothing.
What areas do you serve?
We serve St. Louis County, St. Charles County, and surrounding areas including Florissant, O'Fallon, St. Peters, Chesterfield, Ferguson, Maryland Heights, Hazelwood, Kirkwood, Webster Groves, Ballwin, Bridgeton, and more. Text us your address and we'll confirm!
Do I need to be home?
Nope! Most of our customers aren't home when we come. Just make sure we have access to the yard (gate code, side gate unlocked, etc.) and we'll take care of everything.
What if I want to cancel?
Just text or call us. That's it. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no guilt trips. We believe in earning your business every single visit.
How does the $70/month flat rate work?
One flat monthly payment covers a thorough cleanup once per month. It's the cheapest monthly option in St. Louis — competitors charge $83-$95/month for the same frequency. Great for budget-conscious dog owners.
What about yards with lots of buildup?
No problem! Whether it's a winter's worth of poop or just a few weeks, we handle heavy buildup. Your free first cleanup includes everything, even if the yard is rough. We'll get it clean.
