Questions About Service Areas

What areas does Tidy Tails serve? ▼
Tidy Tails serves 25+ cities across St. Louis County, St. Charles County, and parts of St. Louis City. Our main coverage zones are North County (Florissant, Hazelwood, Ferguson, Bridgeton), South County (Crestwood, Kirkwood, Webster Groves, Mehlville), West County (Chesterfield, Ballwin, Creve Coeur, Wildwood), Central County (Clayton, Ladue, University City, Maplewood), and parts of St. Charles County (O'Fallon, St. Peters, St. Charles). If you're not sure, text your address to (314) 850-7140 and we'll confirm within minutes.
Do you service North County St. Louis (Florissant, Hazelwood, Ferguson)? ▼
Yes — and North County is actually one of our core markets. Florissant (63031, 63033, 63034), Hazelwood (63042), Ferguson (63135), and Bridgeton (63044) are all fully covered. North County is underserved by national franchises, which means we're often the best — and sometimes only — local option. We already have customers in Florissant and actively service the area each week.
Do you service South County (Crestwood, Kirkwood, Webster Groves)? ▼
Yes. We service Crestwood (63126), Kirkwood (63122), Webster Groves (63119), Mehlville (63125), Oakville (63129), Affton, Shrewsbury, and Sunset Hills. South County is a strong service area for us — it's where we participate in community events and have a growing customer base.
Do you service West County (Chesterfield, Ballwin, Creve Coeur)? ▼
Yes — Chesterfield (63005, 63017), Ballwin (63011, 63021), Creve Coeur (63141, 63146), Town & Country (63131), Wildwood (63005, 63038), and Ellisville (63011) are all covered. West County yards tend to be larger, which is exactly why regular service is worth it — there's more ground to cover and more accumulation to manage.
What if my city isn't listed? ▼
If your city is in St. Louis County, there's a strong chance we can service you — especially if you're close to a neighborhood we already cover. Text your address to (314) 850-7140 and we'll let you know quickly. We expand routes based on demand, so if we have neighbors lined up in your area, we'll often add you at no extra charge.
Is there pet waste removal service near me in St. Louis? ▼
If you're searching "pet waste removal near me" in the St. Louis area, you've found it. Tidy Tails is a locally owned pooper scooper service — not a national franchise, not a Craigslist scraper. Jamie (the owner) personally manages every route. Text (314) 850-7140 with your address and we'll confirm service in your area the same day.
Do you charge extra for being outside the main service area? ▼
Our standard flat rate applies for all covered zip codes — $70/month for weekly service regardless of yard size or dog count (up to 4 dogs). For addresses that require significant additional driving outside our main routes, we may ask for a small adjustment — but we'll always tell you upfront before starting service. No hidden fees, ever.
