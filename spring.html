Questions? We Got You.

Do I need to be home?
Nope! Most of our customers aren't. Just make sure we can access your yard and we'll handle everything. You'll get a text when we're done.
Is there really no contract?
Really. Zero contracts, zero cancellation fees, zero BS. You can pause, change, or stop your service anytime with a simple text. We keep customers by being great, not by trapping them.
What happens if it rains?
We still come! Rain, snow, heat — your dogs don't stop, and neither do we. We'll reschedule only if conditions are truly dangerous (ice storms, tornadoes, etc).
I only have one small dog. Is it worth it?
One small dog = about 100 piles per month. That's 100 times you don't have to put on shoes, grab a bag, and do the thing you hate. At $75/mo, that's 75 cents per pile. Your time is worth more than that.
What if my yard is really bad?
That's literally what the Spring Reset Package is for. We've seen it all — months of buildup, multiple dogs, you name it. No judgment. We'll get it spotless and keep it that way.
Do you serve my area?
We cover all of St. Louis County, St. Charles County, and surrounding areas — Florissant, Ferguson, Maryland Heights, St. Ann, O'Fallon, St. Peters, Chesterfield, Ballwin, and more. Text us your zip code and we'll confirm in 2 minutes.
How does the free first cleanup work?
Sign up for weekly service and your very first visit is completely free. No catch. We want you to see the difference before you pay a cent. If you love it (you will), service continues the following week.
