Common Questions

Can I actually get same-day in St. Louis?
Yes — when the schedule allows. Text (314) 850-7140 with your address and we'll tell you within a few minutes whether we can fit you in today. The earlier you text, the better. Morning texts almost always get a same-day slot. Afternoon is hit-or-miss.
How much does a same-day emergency cleanup cost?
One-time cleanups start at $75 for standard yards (1-2 dogs). There's no extra fee for same-day — it's the same price. If you want to start weekly service after, your first regular visit is FREE.
Do I need to be home?
No. As long as we have gate access, we'll handle it. You'll get a text when we're On Our Way and another when we're All Done. Most customers are at work or out running errands when we come.
What do you do with the waste?
We double-bag everything and take it off your property entirely. It doesn't stay in bags at the curb or in your trash cans. It leaves your yard — that's the point.
What if same-day isn't available?
We'll tell you immediately — usually first thing the next morning is doable. We don't leave you guessing. If you need it before Easter and same-day is taken, text as early Friday morning as possible.
Is this just for emergencies, or can I book weekly?
Both. Lots of people book a same-day cleanup first — see the yard clean for the first time in months — and then start weekly service. Weekly is $70/month flat for 1-2 dogs, no contracts. First weekly visit is FREE.
What areas do you serve?
All of St. Louis County (North, South, West, Central) and St. Charles County (O'Fallon, Wentzville, St. Peters, St. Charles). Text your address and we'll confirm your area.
