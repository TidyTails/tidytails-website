Is Pet Waste Service Worth It in St. Charles County?

Honest answer: for most families with fenced yards and dogs in St. Charles County, yes. Here's the decision guide:

It's probably worth it if:

  • You have two working adults in the house and weekends fill up fast
  • You have multiple dogs or a larger yard where accumulation happens quickly
  • You bought your house specifically for the backyard and want to actually use it
  • Your kids want to play outside and you'd rather not supervise every step
  • You've had to deal with HOA complaints or landscaper issues related to pet waste
  • You've looked out the back door on a Friday and felt genuine dread about the weekend cleanup

It's probably fine to DIY if:

  • You have one small dog and a very small yard
  • You're home daily and genuinely stay on top of it
  • Outdoor cleanup is something you actively enjoy and already build into your routine

At $2.30/day, the math favors service for most multi-dog households. The question is really whether consistency and reliability matter to you — and for most of our customers, it does.
